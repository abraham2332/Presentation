Here are a few reasons why coding is important:

  • Automation: With automation becoming increasingly important in many industries, the ability to code is essential for developing and maintaining automated systems.
  • Innovation: Coding is at the heart of many technological innovations, from new mobile apps to cutting-edge artificial intelligence systems.
  • Problem-solving: Coding teaches you how to break down complex problems into smaller, more manageable parts, which can be a valuable skill in many different contexts.
  • Communication: Coding is a universal language that can be used to communicate complex ideas across borders and cultures.
  • Career opportunities: Coding is a highly in-demand skill, and there are many lucrative career opportunities available for skilled coders.
