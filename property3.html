What is Education?

Education is a process of acquiring knowledge, skills, values, and attitudes through a structured and systematic approach. It involves learning from various sources such as teachers, books, experiences, and interactions with others. The primary purpose of education is to prepare individuals for their personal and professional lives, to enable them to contribute positively to society, and to fulfill their potential as human beings.

Education provides individuals with the tools and knowledge they need to navigate the world around them, to make informed decisions, and to pursue their goals and aspirations.
